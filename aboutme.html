About Me

Hi!

Thanks for stopping by and checking out my travels. I hope you enjoy reviewing the destinations I've reviewed and stay tuned as I hope to visit many more. If you have the time check out some of the amazing travel stories from other explorers too.

Love

Love is one of the most importent things in live. Find what you love and enjoy doing and try to do it a little every day. I love to travel and in my free time I'm always planning the next adventure. It's important to make time for the things you love..

Live

Live you life to the fullest is my motto. If you can't work in your dream job just right now that's ok but make sure you live your best life no matter what you do. It's all about enjoying life and making the most of the one we get.

Travel

The reason I made this website was for my love of travel. I'm not sure if it's the fact I grew up on a small island or my love for the sea but I've always had a curiosity about exploring and going out on adventures. If travel is something you enjoy doing make the time for it!
